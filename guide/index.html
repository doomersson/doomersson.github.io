Overview

What is efficiency?

According to the official Tanki Wiki, efficiency (also called effectiveness) is calculated using a non-disclosed formula that reflects how effective a player is in battles. A separate leaderboard tracks the Top 100 players by this metric.

Two key ratios drive your score: your Kill/Death ratio and your Crystal/Experience (C/E) ratio. Keeping both high is essential.
Progression

Rank bonuses

From Rank 1 to Rank 31 (Legend), you earn a flat 3,000 efficiency points per rank — but only if you meet both conditions below:

Kill/Death ratio ≥ 2.0
Crystal/Experience ratio ≥ 1.0
Rank 1 → … → Rank 31 (Legend) → No flat bonus

If your C/E ratio drops below 1.0, the bonus is reduced (typically 2,500–2,800). After Rank 31, the flat rank bonus no longer applies.

Optimal modes and maps

The most efficient play happens on large maps with high player counts, maximizing battle activity and battle fund.

Context

Why the leaderboard is changing

The Top 100 list was mostly frozen from 2021 onwards due to experience inflation — intentionally introduced so players would rank up faster but with weaker equipment. Two recent patches have disrupted this:

Patch #794
March 28, 2025 — Experience gains reduced by 25%. Read patch notes →
Patch #800
May 27, 2025 — Premium Pass crystal bonus increased from +100% to +150%. Read patch notes →
